Learning About Lemurs

I think lemurs are the greatest! Here are some things I love about them:

  • There are over 110 species of lemur.
  • Lemurs are native to Madagascar. That is the only country where they exist in the wild!
  • They have expressive eyes and faces.
  • They are very agile

Almost every species of lemur is endangered.
